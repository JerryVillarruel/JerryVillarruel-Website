I am a healthcare assistant, health professional who helps assist other health professionals.I have the qualifications for the skills, studies, and interest in this desire to work. I am certified in American Heart Association Basic Life Support cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR), American Heart Association First-Aid, Community Emergency Response Team (CERT), Electro Cardio Gram (ECG), and Injections. .

My usual work area is at a clinical space. I tend to do a lot of vital signs as well communicating with patients. The usual work is updating and filling patient medical records, coding and filling out insurance forms, arranging hospital admissions and laboratory services, making appointments, making phone calls, getting patients ready for physicians, and explaining things to them so they can walk out knowing what they need to know.

I started my journey in freshmen year of highschool from getting the basic certifications to shadowing health care providers to having an internship at a clinic. I volunteered at several clinics as well as hospitals trying to observe and gain as much knowledge as I can obtain. This helped me dive in to the medical field more and become a medical assistant.
